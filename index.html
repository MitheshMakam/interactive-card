🍫 Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: 45 Minutes

Servings: 6 People

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Flour
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Cocoa Powder
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1/2 cup Butter

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Add eggs, milk and butter.
  3. Whisk until smooth.
  4. Pour into baking tray.
  5. Bake for 35 minutes.
  6. Cool and decorate.
